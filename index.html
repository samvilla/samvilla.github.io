Going into the 4 tile which was inspired by Kyle who was determined to pass United States geography but failed to find New York. Then comes a girl Neha being the perfect fit for the 8 tile by correcting the phase indians to Native Americans any time she got the chance. With all these silly people there had to be someone to kill all the jokes our 16 tile told jokes so horribly she earned the name Jayus, which in Indonesian stood for 'bad joke teller'. Then there is always someone who takes jokes to far Mathew became known as racist making him the 32 tile. Emily a.k.a Kenny followed Mathew, with tile 64, due to her strange sense of cruel humor. Followed by a person who is never here to experience the humor Rocio, tile 128, nicknamed Oscar because she is following in her brother's footsteps. In tile 256, Nicole Warlord a close friend to Rocio. Tile 512 was Lincoln because I needed a smart kid to be in this game and the guy seemed pretty smart. Mr.Wassmuth, tile 1024, was nicknamed tear maker because he made Katie the Crier cry in tile 2048. The End. Well until AP Econ next year...

How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same value touch, they merge into one!
